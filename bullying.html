I saw my friend being bullied the other day. What should I do?


Whenever someone gets bullied, and you've seen it happen, you obviously have two options: to expose the bully and complain, or to approach the bully.
Both options are scary, but to actually eliminate wrong from this world, you need to be brave.
First, approach the bully. Tell him/her what he/she's doing is wrong. Explain why. Educate him/her on basic respect and human rights. If the bully still doesn't seem to agree, try talking to him/her to find out their motivation behind bullying: because all bullies have been bullied. Assure the bully that they are not alone, and they can't help themselves by bullying others.
If they still don't understand, then approach an authority figure. You will NOT look like a coward for doing so, you are doing the right thing. Remember that.
